Science

In biology class with Mr. Shafer we learned about genetics and as a final assignment I built a model of a DNA molecule. I used wire and hot glue and Craft foam to make it. It shows the nucleotides in their pairs, the double helix structure.

I also had some experiences planning and running school dances. It all seems so long ago now, but I know it has really only been a few weeks.

Immersion: Abstract Art

I made this abstract art in Mr. Russ' week-long abstract art (and philosophy) immersion. I was not initially planning on this being a major piece from the class, it started as just a little doodle, but it turned out to be one of my favorite peices I have made so far. It is interesting how the two figures feel like they have emotions and personalities despite just being weird shapes.

Immersion: Music Production

Here is a little tune that I made in 3/4 time during Me Thorne's week-long music production immersion using the software soundtrap. I initially developed the chord progression on my electric guitar and then recreated it digitally. If you listen to the whole thing there is a guitar solo that starts about half way through that I actually played on the keyboard. This piece was created before I knew very much about music production, and listening back I know I could do better if I had it to do over. But this is an example of my work from that time and shows me how far I have progressed as a musical creator.
