About Me

I am Sterling, I'm 14, and music is the center of my life. I play the bass, the guitar, the drums, and a little bit of keyboard. My favorite thing about playing the bass guitar is how thick the strings are and the low register, it sounds realty nice to me and its my favorite instrument. The main influences on the music that I am making right now are are Bent Knee, Radiohead, Thundercat and Victor Wooten (I also really enjoy Tool ... a LOT. Lucky me I got to see them live before CoViD-19 took every one off the road). Going forward I hope to become an internet muscician that makes music for others to listen to and enjoy.
